Who We Are

Veda Lighting Design is an independent Lighting Design consultancy offering its services across the Middle East and India through its offices at Dubai - United Arab Emirates, and Bangalore and Kochi in India. VEDA reaches forth to clients across major cities through a team of creative designers and lighting professionals. Our expertise covers a wide variety of projects that include homes of High-Net-worth Individuals, Shopping Malls, High rise buildings, Townships and Villa developments, Clubhouses, Outdoor landscapes lighting, Building facades and Commercial Interiors.

We offer clients the very best in global best practices in lighting design and sustainability that are firmly rooted in Iocal realities and ground level project execution.

Ranjith K Kartha is the Founder & Principal Designer at Veda and leads its design and growth efforts. He brings over two decades of entrepreneurial experience, in lighting design and product development, in India, the UAE and the USA.

He holds a Master’s degree in Lighting from the world's leading school in lighting, the Lighting Research Center (LRC) at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), Troy, New York. He also holds a post graduate certificate in Business Management from XLRI, India and a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Bharathiyar University, India.

He is passionate about teaching and has delivered talks and workshops at various professional institutions such as SJB school of Architecture & Planning, Bangalore, National Institute of Design (NID), Bangalore and Srishti School of Design, Bangalore.

WHAT WE DO

VLD’s lighting designs are emotive, contemporary and technologically current. Light is a controllable tool of space design, just as form, scale and material. Light translates vision, and it is vision that gives us a major portion of our experiences At Veda, we reinforce the designed environment by making light our ally in the translation of the space.

Our design practice encompasses the following:

  • Daylight design
  • Interior lighting
  • Exterior facade lighting
  • Landscape lighting
  • Roadway lighting
  • Lighting efficiency audits
  • Custom luminaire design and
  • Light art

Veda offers a truly independent lighting design consultancy experience.

To the building developer, our energy efficient lighting design provides visual branding to the space resulting in an enhanced customer experience, higher perceived value and a lower annual cost of operation. We are experts in lighting technology and ensure that our specified products meet strict lighting objectives, quality norms, efficiency standards and aesthetic appeal. We lay utmost emphasis in protecting the Architect’s vision and the client’s interests through an independent and un-biased recommendation of light fixtures.

Further, our lighting design goes beyond light planning services provided by luminaire manufacturers. Though Illuminance (lux) planning aids functional tasks, it falls short in the holistic integration of light and architecture. Veda supports Architects from the initial concept stage of the project by articulating light into the architectural fiber of the building and thereby evolving a distinctive character to its form and the space within the building. Our early involvement ensures that the lighting load of the building is reduced to the minimum while enhancing occupant comfort, increasing day lit spaces and meeting sustainability and green building goals.

Our team brings together experts from different backgrounds including architecture, engineering, theatre, and product design. Our years of collective experience allows us to service every aspect of architectural lighting from overall space definition and process illumination to the design of an individual luminaire. Our designs are imaginative, pragmatic, and come with an exemplary level of service.

PROCESS

Our process is divided into six phases such as

Stage 1:
Programming phase
Schematic design

Stage 2:
Design Development
Construction documents

Stage 3:
Construction Administration
Post-occupancy verification

In Stage-1, programming and schematic design phase, we conduct extensive research of the project by site visits, historical and cultural references, geographic influences and the demographic profile of the occupants as well as in the neighboring areas. We then communicate our lighting design concepts to the core architectural team through a variety of visual media, such as hand sketches, photographs, mockups, storyboards, 3D modeling and other visualization techniques. Also, preliminary estimates of the project’s economic metrics such as light fixture cost budgets, efficiency targets and overall sustainability goals are developed.

After the design concept is frozen, in Stage-2, design development and construction documents phase, a definite set of documents are created that includes lighting schedules and load calculations, luminaire specifications, cut sheets of recommended light fixtures, cost budgets and the lighting control drawings.

In the final Stage 3, our team works with the Architect, the contractor and the manufacturers in ensuring the specification integrity of the design. Alternative product submittals, if so recommended by the contractor, are verified for design consistency with the original design intent in performance, aesthetics, life and availability of the product. Further, our designers also ensure that the light fixtures are placed and aimed accurately during the post-occupancy validation process. A punch list is created that record any anomalies in the installation and would recommend corrective measures.
